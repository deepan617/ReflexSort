About ReflexSort

Where AI Meets Sustainability

ReflexSort is an intelligent, fully automated waste sorting system that fuses computer vision, deep learning, and robotic actuation to classify and sort recyclable materials at industrial scale — without any human intervention.

Designed for recycling plants, smart cities, and manufacturing facilities, ReflexSort operates continuously, adapts to new waste categories, and delivers consistent, measurable environmental impact.
